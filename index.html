I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Joint Center for Quantum Information and Computer Science (QuICS) at the University of Maryland, College Park. I completed my PhD at Harvard, supervised by Misha Lukin. My BS is from Caltech, where I was influenced by the folks at IQIM.


- Email: ekhabibo@umd.edu.
- Office: 3353 Atlantic Building.

Research

I work on theory for quantum computing and quantum many-body physics. During my PhD, I focused on communication protocols and thermalization. I also collaborate with experiment on a quantum optics platform. My new interests are in algorithms for quantum computation and simulation.
