I'm a PhD student in the Department of Physics at Harvard University, fortunate to be advised by Mikhail Lukin. I received a BS in Physics from Caltech in 2016.

Research Interests

I work on theory between quantum computing and quantum many-body physics, which bridges traditional boundaries between quantum information science, atomic molecular and optical physics, and condensed matter. In particular, I am intested in communication protocols and thermalizationm, with some connections to complexity theory and holography.
