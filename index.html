I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Physics at Harvard, supervised by Mikhail Lukin. I obtained a BS in Physics from Caltech in 2016, where I was influenced by the folks at IQIM.
- Email: ekhabiboulline@g.harvard.edu.

Research Interests

I work on theory for quantum computing and quantum many-body physics. In particular, I am interested in communication protocols and thermalization.
