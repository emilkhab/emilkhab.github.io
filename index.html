I'm a PhD student in the Department of Physics at Harvard, supervised by Mikhail Lukin. My BS is in Physics from Caltech, where I was influenced by the folks at IQIM.

- Email: ekhabiboulline@g.harvard.edu.

Research

I work on theory for quantum computing and quantum many-body physics. In particular, I am interested in communication protocols and thermalization. I also look for realizations on a quantum optics platform.
